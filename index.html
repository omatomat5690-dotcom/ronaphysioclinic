Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a GP referral?

No. You can book directly without a referral. If you have been referred by a GP or consultant, please bring any relevant documentation to your first session.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by condition. Some patients improve within 2–3 sessions, while more complex conditions may require a longer course. You will receive an honest assessment after your first appointment.

What happens in the first session?

Your first session includes a detailed assessment of your condition, a discussion of your goals, and the start of a tailored treatment plan. Treatment typically begins on the same day.

Can I book a same-day appointment?

Yes, same-day appointments are available subject to availability. Contact via WhatsApp for the quickest response.

Do you offer home visits?

Yes, home visits are available in selected areas across London including Enfield, Southgate, Palmers Green, Wood Green, Barnet, and Haringey.

What techniques do you use?

Treatment may include manual therapy, joint mobilisation and manipulation, muscle energy techniques, dry needling, electrotherapy, taping, cupping therapy, lymphatic drainage, and tailored exercise programmes.
